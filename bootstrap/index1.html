Meet the Admirals!

All the admirals in One Piece!

Kizaru (Borsalino)

Glint-Glint Fruit (Pika Pika no Mi) - Logia type, grants him the ability to move at the speed of light and generate and manipulate light.

Akainu (Sakazuki)

Magma-Magma Fruit (Magu Magu no Mi) - Logia type, allows him to create, control, and transform into magma.

Aokiji (Kuzan)

Ice-Ice Fruit (Hie Hie no Mi) - Logia type, allows him to create, control, and transform into ice.

Fujitora (Issho)

Gravity-Gravity Fruit (Zushi Zushi no Mi) - Paramecia type, allows him to manipulate gravity at will, which he uses to create powerful gravitational forces.

Ryokugyu (Aramaki)

Nature-Nature Fruit (Mori Mori no Mi) - Logia type, allows him to control and transform into plant life, which he uses for both offense and defense.
